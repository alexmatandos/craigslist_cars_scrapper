Stunning 1982 Morgan Plus 8 Roadster in beautiful Indigo Blue paint with contrasting dark Oxblood red leather interior and only 14,500 original miles since new.

This particular Morgan was purchased by an American gentleman while in the UK on vacation. He dropped by a prominent Morgan dealership and was so impressed with them that he decided to purchase one on the spot. He took delivery of the car and after having enjoyed it while he was there proceeded to have this beautiful RHD version shipped back to the US.

The current condition of this Morgan is short of perfect, having been driven so few miles while also receiving newer paint and many upgrades along the way. The exterior finish shines like it was painted yesterday and a blemish would be almost impossible to find. The weather equipment (top, tonneau and side curtains) is all matching blue canvas and fits like a glove. The convertible top windows are clear and as new. Chrome and glass are in excellent condition. Factory Plus 8 wheels are essentially flawless and riding on like new Pirelli 205/60 R15 Radials. Chrome luggage rack, wood steering wheel, driving lights, wind wings and grille badges just add to the English magic.

The Plus 8 is the highest performance version of the Morgan line-up and this one does not disappoint. The Rover V8 is very strong and sounds fantastic due to the long tube headers and dual stainless steel exhaust. Intake and fuel management have both been upgraded for better performance. Mated to a 5 speed manual transmission and less than 2,000 lbs weight makes this car a real joy to drive. All systems, lights, gauges, etc. work exactly as they should.

Whether you are looking for one to drive regularly, only on nice days, or simply something to take to your local car shows, I do not believe you can find a better driving more attractive example with so few original miles.
